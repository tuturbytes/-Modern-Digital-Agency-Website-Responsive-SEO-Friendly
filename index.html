Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and processes.

How long does it take to see results from SEO?

SEO is a long-term strategy, and results typically start becoming noticeable within 3-6 months. However, this timeline can vary depending on your industry competition, website age, and the current state of your SEO. We provide monthly reports to track progress and make adjustments as needed.

What's included in your monthly marketing services?

Our monthly services include strategy development, campaign implementation, performance monitoring, and regular reporting. The specific services depend on your chosen plan but may include SEO, PPC, social media management, content creation, email marketing, and analytics. We tailor our services to your specific business goals.

Do you work with businesses in my industry?

We've worked with clients across various industries including e-commerce, SaaS, healthcare, legal, real estate, and more. Our strategies are customized for each industry's unique challenges and opportunities. Even if we haven't worked in your specific niche before, our data-driven approach ensures we can deliver results.

How do you measure marketing success?

We establish clear KPIs (Key Performance Indicators) based on your business objectives. These may include website traffic, lead generation, conversion rates, customer acquisition cost, return on ad spend, or revenue growth. We provide transparent reporting so you can see exactly how your marketing dollars are performing.

Can I cancel my contract at any time?

We offer flexible contract terms. Our standard agreement requires a 30-day notice for cancellation, but there are no long-term lock-in contracts. We believe in earning your business every month through results, not through contractual obligations.
